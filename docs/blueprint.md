# **App Name**: Kapendeka Family Hub

## Core Features:

- Family Dashboard & Unified Portal Access: A centralized UI providing an overview of upcoming events, tasks, and recent family activity, with easy navigation to all dedicated portals (e.g., Household, Work, School, Health, Finances, Pets, Hobbies, Emergency).
- Shared & Color-Coded Calendar: A collaborative family calendar for scheduling all events (work, school, church, health, travel), managing recurring occurrences, integrating public holidays/school terms, and setting reminders.
- Dynamic Chores, To-Do Lists & Gamification: Manage private and shared to-do lists, and recurring household chores with smart assignment rotation, automated points for completion, and a gamification system (points, streaks, badges, rewards).
- AI-Powered Meal Planner & Smart Shopping List Tool: Plan family meals using a recipe box, and leverage an AI tool to automatically generate categorized grocery shopping lists, accepting natural language input for items and meal suggestions.
- Family Communication & Journaling: A hub for sharing family posts (text, photos, media), notes, memos, and maintaining a family journal, alongside private and channel-based messaging.
- Financial Tracking & Management: Track family expenses, allowances, and transactions, providing financial oversight with support for specific currencies (e.g., ZAR).
- Secure Family Vault & Health Profiles: A secure digital vault for important family documents (e.g., insurance, medical IDs) with role-based access, alongside dedicated health profiles for family members.
- Personalized Settings & Member Management: Granular role-based permissions, user-specific preferences (e.g., notifications, theme), opt-in location sharing, and a robust onboarding/invitation system.
- Celebration & Reminder System: Track and manage family birthdays, anniversaries, and other celebrations with automated reminders and planning aids.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Muted indigo-violet (#553D7A). This rich, calming hue evokes stability and togetherness, grounding the app's 'Universe Hub' feel without being overly dark for a family-focused, light theme.
- Background color: Soft lavender-white (#F7F5F9). A heavily desaturated and very bright version of the primary color, creating a clean, serene canvas that supports legibility and visual comfort for all ages.
- Accent color: Vibrant sky blue (#3E93F4). This bright, analogous color provides a clear contrast, drawing attention to calls-to-action, highlights, and important information, representing clarity and fresh beginnings.
- Headline and Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif). A highly legible and neutral font that ensures clarity and accessibility for all family members, from children to elders, supporting the app's 'simple to advanced' scaling.
- Use modern, clean line icons that are easily recognizable and consistently styled across all features, facilitating intuitive navigation for diverse age groups.
- Adopt a responsive, modular layout featuring a central, personalized dashboard with quick access to key family updates, complemented by clearly structured portal views for detailed management.
- Implement subtle and purposeful animations for transitions, feedback upon action completion, and dynamic updates, enhancing the user experience without causing distraction.
